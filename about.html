About ECU Enhancements

ECU Enhancements is a Melbourne-based mobile ECU tuning service specialising in safe, reliable and dyno-informed performance upgrades.

Our Approach

We combine data-driven calibration with real-world experience to deliver tunes that feel strong, smooth and predictable. Every vehicle is treated as a system – engine, driveline and use case are all considered before any changes are made.

Vehicles We Work With

  • Passenger cars and performance builds
  • 4WDs and touring rigs
  • Light and heavy commercial vehicles
  • Motorcycles
  • Marine and jet skis

Our Values

  • Safety and reliability first
  • Transparent communication
  • Tailored solutions, not generic files
  • Long-term customer relationships
